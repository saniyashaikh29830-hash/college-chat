🎓 Admission & Courses

The college offers courses like B.Com, BMS, BAF, BBI, BSc IT, BSc CS, BA, and some postgraduate programs like M.Com.

Admission is based on merit (12th marks) through the Mumbai University online admission process followed by document verification.

What documents are required?
Required documents include 10th & 12th marksheets, leaving certificate, Aadhaar card, passport photos, caste certificate (if applicable).

What is the fee structure?
Fees vary by course, generally ranging from approx ₹5,000 to ₹25,000 per year for aided/unaided courses.

Are scholarships available?
Yes, government and minority scholarships are available for eligible students.
